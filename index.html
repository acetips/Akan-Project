Akan Name Generator

by Emelda Perez

Description:

The Akan Name Generator acquaints you as a user of your Akan name based on your gender and birth details. The Akan is a language group in Ghana, that names its members based on the day of the week on which an individual was born. According to the Akan, these names have significant meanings. Therefore, upon entering your birth details, this web application auto generates your Akan name. Click here to learn more about the Akan people.
